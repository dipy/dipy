DIPY is a part of the NiPy community and we are happy to share the same e-mail list. This makes sense as we can share ideas with a broader community and boost collaboration across neuroimagers. If you want to post to the list you will need first to subscribe to the nipy mailing list. We suggest to begin the subject of your e-mail with [DIPY]. This will help us respond faster to your questions.

Additional help can be found on the Neurostars website, or on the dipy gitter channel.
